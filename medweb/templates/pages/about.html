  1. Synergy+ History

    January 16th, 2016

    CEO John Oljace comes back from the Phillipines and reunites with ex-employee Paul Lukey. Paul's existing relationship with visionary Steve Porter (with his healthcare software company NovoMedici) leads to an excited discussion. A 7 month research endeavor begins, with the mission of improving a tumultuous healthcare industry.
