  1. Synergy+ History

    November 16th, 2015

    After 22 years as a CEO in Physician Recruiting and 20 years assisting 4 doctors in treating chronic diseases, John Oljace wanted to find a business model where he could combine his personal and business interests. John started extensive research covering all sectors of healthcare to plan out his next business venture.

  2. Old Friends Reunite

    February 20th, 2016

    John reunited with Paul Lukey, ex-employee and friend of 22 years, to discuss industry problems and solutions. Paul disclosed he spent 15 years in secret developing a superior IT Health system with visionary Dr. Steve Porter.

  3. Partnership

    March 3rd, 2016

  4. A Healthcare Vision

    April 1st, 2016

    John reached out to top diagnostic physician and mentor Dr. Paul Herscu to discuss plans to reduce chronic disease. John becomes aware of the dire need for Population Health Management and evidenced based treatment methods.

  5. Change of Approach

    June 5th, 2016

    MD Revolution now changes it's name to dba (doing business as) Synergy+ Value Based Services. The core team of experts with over 145 years in healthcare publishing, consulting, management, recruiting, and clinical knowledge was assembled.

  6. The Business Man

    July 12th, 2016

    Jonah Schmiechen joins Synergy+ as COO, and begins developement of a comprehensive and articulate business plan.

  7. A Meeting by Chance

    August 12th, 2016

    John happens to meet Ex-Cerner employee James Audretsch. James comes aboard as CIO and begins development of the website. He and John Oljace develop an online marketing plan to dominate Google Pay Per Click. Team meetings commence to work on the goal of being fully operational by January 2017.

  8. Writing Mentor

    November 3rd, 2016

    Plans for marketing were finalized as mentor Randal Shields, a top newspaper publisher & editor with experience in growing businesses, joined as chief editor.

  9. The Race Begins

    October 3rd, 2016

    All work was completed to formulate a business plan, and the strategy to secure funds in exchange for equity was deployed.

  10. Initial Investment

    November 7th, 2016

    CPA Dave Sare evaluated Synergy+'s business plans. Within minutes he recognized distinct advantages over competitors and became the inital investor.
