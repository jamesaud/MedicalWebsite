Stock and Investment

For our first round of funding we will secure loans totalling $150,000 from friends, family and business partners. It is critical to raise money before Thanksgivingto analyze Google PPC (pay per click) prior to January, the start of our busy season.

Lenders may; convert notes to Synergy+ stock upon completion of a private placement memorandum (PPM) required for non-registered securities. We should have this completed by mid-January.
